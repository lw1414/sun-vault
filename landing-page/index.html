Good questions

The things smart buyers ask.

Is the first call really free?+

Yes — the Solar Savings & Design Call costs nothing and carries no obligation. You leave it knowing what's possible for your specific roof and bills, whether or not you work with us.

What makes SunVault Live different from other apps?+

Most installers hand you a generic portal, if anything. SunVault Live is watt-level and proactive: we monitor your system 24/7 and reach out the moment anything underperforms, so you never leave savings on the table.

How much does a system cost?+

Most premium homes land between $3,200 and $12,150 (₱180,000–₱680,000) depending on size, battery, and whether you add EV charging. We show you the exact number — and the payback period — on your design call, before you commit to anything.

Will the panels look good on my home?+

That's a design decision, not an afterthought. We use all-black premium panels and lay them out for how your home reads from the street, not just for install convenience.

What if something goes wrong after install?+

Your dedicated engineer stays your point of contact, and SunVault Live means we usually spot issues before you do. Systems are covered by a 25-year performance warranty.
