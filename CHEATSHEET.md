## 🎬 Loom — client presentation (the hero deliverable, ~4–6 min)

Present *as SunVault's agency to the client.* Suggested flow:
1. **Name the business + the promise** — "This is SunVault: luxury solar you can actually watch
   work. See exactly what you save, down to the watt."
2. **The ad** — show the carousel + the Facebook ad draft. "This is what a stranger sees."
3. **The landing page** — tour it, click **Book a call** → the calendar opens.
4. **The pipeline** — show the populated board + total value. "Every lead lives here."
5. **The engine** — show a pretty email, then the automations. Run a test booking → lead lands
   on the board + confirmation email fires. "Nobody clicks send. The system does."
6. **Close** — "That's the whole system: ad → page → pipeline → automations, all on-brand."

## 🎥 Raven — Day 5 instructor update (behind-the-scenes, ~2–4 min)

Different from the Loom — this is *your* build update, not a client pitch:
- What you built and the order (pipeline → page → carousel/ad → emails → automations).
- One thing you're proud of (the live-monitor hero / the branded email system).
- One thing that was tricky and how you solved it.
- What you'd add with more time (A4 hot-lead router, SMS, more proof).
