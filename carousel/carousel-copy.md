# Instagram Carousel — SunVault

**Goal:** stop the scroll, land the "you can't see your solar working" insight, and drive a tap
to the landing page → book a call. 6 slides, one consistent brand set.

**Visual system (every slide):**
- Background: midnight navy `#0B1F2A` (deep, night-sky).
- Accent: solar amber `#F5A623` for the key word / underline; live green `#3DDC97` for "live" data only.
- Fonts: Space Grotesk (headline), Inter (body), mono for any data readout.
- A tiny amber sun mark + "SunVault" wordmark bottom-left on every slide.
- 4:5 portrait (1080×1350) for feed carousel.

---

## Slide 1 — Hook
**Headline:** Your solar is either saving you a fortune… or quietly failing. **Would you even know?**
**Visual:** dark navy, a faint solar panel grid, one panel glowing amber, the rest dim. Live-green "?" pulse.
**Caption chip:** LUXURY SOLAR THAT THINKS

## Slide 2 — The blind spot
**Headline:** Most solar owners are **flying blind.**
**Body:** Panels go up, the crew drives off, and you never really see what it's doing — or catch it when it stops.
**Visual:** a dashboard with the screen switched off / static — "no signal".

## Slide 3 — The reframe
**Headline:** What if you could **see every watt** — live?
**Body:** SunVault Live shows production, usage, battery, and dollars saved in real time. Proof, not promises.
**Visual:** the live monitor readout (like the site hero): `6.42 kW` in amber, green LIVE dot, small bar chart.

## Slide 4 — The savings proof
**Headline:** **₱90,000 a year**, and you watch it add up.
**Body:** Typical SunVault home runs 82% off the grid — and sees the savings tick up daily in the app.
**Visual:** big amber number, a rising green line, "SAVED THIS YEAR" mono label.

## Slide 5 — Why it's different
**Headline:** One engineer. **Lifetime monitoring.** Zero guessing.
**Body:** The same person designs, installs, and switches on your system — then we watch it for life and reach out before you'd ever notice a dip.
**Visual:** three clean amber icons (person, shield, live pulse) with short labels.

## Slide 6 — CTA
**Headline:** See what **your roof** could save.
**Body:** Book a free 30-minute Solar Savings & Design Call. Real numbers for your home.
**Button chip (amber):** Book your call → link in bio
**Visual:** bold amber CTA pill on navy, sun mark, confident white space.
