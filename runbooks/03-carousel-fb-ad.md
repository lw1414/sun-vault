# Runbook 03 — Carousel + Facebook Ad (DRAFT)

**Goal:** a finished on-brand Instagram carousel and a Facebook ad saved as a **draft**
pointing to your landing page. Screenshot into `D5/`.

---

## A. The carousel slides (already rendered)

Six on-brand slides are in `carousel/`: `slide-01.png … slide-06.png` (1080×1350).
Copy is in `carousel/carousel-copy.md`, including the Instagram caption.

- Review the six PNGs. Want changes (wording, a different stat, a 7th slide)? Ask me — the
  slides are built from `carousel/slide-N.html` + `carousel/_slides.css`, so I can re-render.
- 📸 **Screenshot →** `D5/part3-carousel-set.png` (show all 6 together, e.g. in a folder
  thumbnail grid or lined up).

*(Optional — post it for real on Instagram: upload the 6 images as a carousel, paste the
caption from `carousel-copy.md`, and put your landing link in your bio. Not required for the
capstone; the ad is what matters.)*

## B. Build the Facebook ad — as a DRAFT

Meta Ads Manager, same Page + ad account as the Facebook Ads module.

1. **Ads Manager → Create** → objective **Traffic** (link clicks).
2. Campaign/ad set: you can leave budget/audience at defaults — **you will not publish**.
3. At the **Ad** level:
   - **Format:** Carousel → upload `slide-01…06.png` in order (or single image = slide 4).
   - **Primary text / Headline / Description:** copy from `ad/facebook-ad.md`.
   - **Website URL:** your deployed landing page link from `brief.md`.
   - **CTA button:** Book Now.
4. Watch the live ad **preview** update on the right — it should look on-brand.
5. **Do NOT click Publish.** Close/exit so it saves as a **Draft** (Ads Manager keeps drafts),
   or use "Close" → "Save as draft" if prompted.
6. 📸 **Screenshot →** `D5/part3-fb-ad-draft.png` (show the preview + the Draft status).

> **Safe mode:** no budget spent, no card added, never published. If Meta nudges you to add
> payment, you've gone a step too far — back out; a draft needs none of it.

---

### ✅ Done when
- A finished 6-slide carousel exists, consistent with the brand.
- A Facebook ad exists **as a draft**, creative = your carousel, destination = landing link.
- Nothing is published; no spend, no card.

*Stand out:* save 2–3 draft variations (hook A vs B, carousel vs single image) — see the
variations list in `ad/facebook-ad.md`.
