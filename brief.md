## Who you are (identity)

**Business:** SunVault — a luxury residential solar + battery company for high-end Philippine
homes, whose systems come with a lifetime IoT monitoring platform. We don't just install
panels; we install a power plant you can watch, tune, and trust from your phone. (Serving
premium homes across Metro Manila, Cebu, and Davao — where Meralco bills bite hardest.)

**What you stand for:** Most homeowners go solar and then fly blind — they never really know
if it's working or how much it saves. SunVault exists to end that. We believe if you're going
to put a power plant on your roof, you should be able to *see it work* — every watt, every
dollar, every day. Ownership you can watch.

**What makes you different:** Two things. (1) **The SunVault Live app** — real-time,
watt-level monitoring of production, home usage, battery health, and dollars saved, with
alerts the moment anything underperforms. (2) **White-glove, no-outsourcing install** — one
dedicated design engineer from first call to switch-on, not a rotating crew and a call
center. Most installers disappear after the truck leaves. We're the one you still hear from
in year five.

---

## What you want (the goal)

**The goal:** Booked "Solar Savings & Design Calls" that turn into signed installs. The whole
system exists to move a stranger from an ad to a booked call to a closed sale.

**Who it is for:** Affluent Filipino homeowners (household income ₱45,000+/month), who own a
larger home in a major metro (Metro Manila, Cebu, Davao), pay ₱9,000+/month in Meralco/
electricity, and care about both savings and having a beautiful, well-run home. They research
before they buy and distrust pushy solar sales.

**The offer:** A premium solar + battery system, fully designed and installed by one dedicated
engineer, with the SunVault Live monitoring app included for life.

**One promise:** **See exactly what you save — down to the watt — from day one.**
